About Us

WMOIG COLLEGE, Cherukattoor, Wayanad is committed to enrich the young generation by empowering them. We provide holistic education that enables them to actively participate in community life. As we know, education is a crucial factor that contributes to nation-building, so for the past ninety years we have been educating young people from different strata of our society. We established our institution by keeping in mind the exact concept of learning to meet the needs of the present generation and we equip them with the necessary skill sets to meet the different challenges in life.
As the mission of WMO organization, WMOIGAC has been laid the foundation by fostering a culture of responsibility, so that students engage with the world around them to take on responsibilities while at college and pursue roles that seek to make a positive contribution after graduation.
